About Me

Hi! I'm Tushar Goyal, born and raised in Mount Abu. I am currently based in Pune, India.
I completed my Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science from Arya College of Engineering & Information Technology in Jaipur.
Currently employed as a Senior React Developer with 9+ years of experience. Hands-on UI/UX design and development experience. Skilled Web UI Developer adept at reducing end-user work to navigate the software. Working knowledge of Agile and waterfall methodologies.
Rather than simply presenting talents, I like to define myself by the type of work I wish to do based on my personality. I'd rather keep learning, putting myself in new situations, experimenting with new yet exciting things, and progressing despite setbacks.
I enjoy being a free thinker. My passion for travelling, dancing, playing guitar, and recording candid moments on camera grew even stronger as a result of this.
I enjoy being socially involved as well as being passionate about assisting others in any manner I can. Life is all about how you see your environment, and I am convinced that a good attitude toward any part of life will shape our character in the same way.
