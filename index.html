About Me

Hi! I'm Tushar Goyal, born and brought up in Mount Abu. Currently staying in Pune, India.
I completed my Bachelors of Technology from Arya College Of Engineering & Information Technology, Jaipur in Computer Science Branch.
Currently working as a senior engineer with experience of around 5 years. Hands-on experience in UI/UX designing and development. Skilled Web UI Developer adept at reducing the amount of work required of end-users to navigate the software. Experience to work on Agile and waterfall methodology.
I like defining myself by the work I want to pursue based on my personality rather than just mere presentation of skills. I prefer to keep learning, challenging myself with various situations, experimenting on different but interesting things and growing despite failure.
I love being a free spirit. This made my passion for travelling, dancing, capturing candid moments on camera and playing guitar grow more.
I like being socially active and also to be passionate about helping people in whatever way I can. Life is all about how you perceive your surroundings and I strongly believe that a positive outlook on any aspect of life will shape our character accordingly.
